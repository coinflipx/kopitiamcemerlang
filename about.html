Who are we?

Welcome to Kopitiam Cemerlang

Where tradition meets taste in a heartwarming blend of flavours. Established with love and a passion for authentic culinary experiences, our kopitiam is more than just a place to enjoy a cup of coffee - it's a journey through the rich tapestry of Malaysian heritage.

A Labor of Love

Founded in 2022, Kopitiam Cemerlang is a labor of love.

Passionately brought to life by a dedicated team. With a commitment to preserving the authencity of traditional kopitiams while adding a modern touch, our story is one of warmth, community, and culinary excellence.
